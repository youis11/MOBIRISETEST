The team

Magical Muffin is a videogame company originated in the subject of "Project 3" in the third year of the university course in Terrassa, Catalonia, Spain, called: Video Game Design and Development in CITM (UPC). We are a group of thirty-seven students full of illusion to make a great game. We already have participated in other smalls projects at university and also out of it. It is our first game that we make together as a whole group and will we the biggest game we will made since now. Our objective is to make a funny game that makes you remember it for a long time.
